About Amali Residences

Amali Residences is one of Dubai's most exceptional ultra-luxury towers, rising 54 storeys directly above the Dubai Water Canal in Al Wasl. Architecture is by Killa Design, creators of the Museum of the Future, with interiors by HBA Residential. Only 211 homes across 2-bedroom residences to sky penthouses, private podium villas and triplex layouts. Every single residence includes a private swimming pool. Defining features include a 5.5-metre floor-to-floor height, offering proportions more akin to a private villa than an apartment tower.

Key Amenities

  • Private pool in every single residence
  • 5.5-metre floor-to-floor heights throughout
  • Architecture by Killa Design (Museum of the Future)
  • Interiors by HBA Residential
  • Infinity pools, spa, sauna, yoga and meditation studios
  • Private cinema, co-working spaces and concierge
  • Podium villas, sky penthouses and triplex layouts available
  • Direct canal frontage with park and skyline views

Everly Place is the newest residential launch from Ellington Properties, arriving in Mohammed Bin Rashid City's Meydan Horizon district. Set directly beside the development's crystal lagoon, the project brings Ellington's design led approach to one of Dubai's fastest growing and most well connected freehold communities. With 209 residences across 13 floors and a full suite of resort style amenities, Everly Place has quickly become one of the most discussed new launches in Dubai this year.

Why Everly Place Is Generating Strong Early Interest

A trusted, design led developer

Ellington Properties has built a strong reputation across Dubai Creek Harbour, Jumeirah Village Circle and Sobha Hartland for thoughtfully designed, detail focused residential buildings. Everly Place continues that philosophy, with interiors and shared spaces shaped around natural light, considered materials and curated art installations woven throughout the development. For buyers who value design quality over generic finishes, Ellington's track record carries real weight.

A genuine lagoon front address

Everly Place sits directly beside the crystal lagoon of Meydan Horizon, a 39 million square foot masterplanned district by Meydan Group. Terraced residences are oriented toward the water and the wider city skyline, with full height glazing and outdoor living spaces designed to draw the lagoon views into daily life. Lagoon front positioning within a freehold community at this stage of launch is genuinely rare in Dubai's current off plan market.

Resort scale amenities

The amenities at Everly Place go well beyond a standard pool and gym. Residents will have access to a 23 metre infinity edge leisure pool, a dedicated kids' pool and play area, a hotel style club lounge with a first of its kind bowling alley and billiards area, a coworking and gaming zone, a fully equipped fitness studio, a spa inspired changing area with sauna and chromotherapy showers, an outdoor cinema, table tennis and shaded beachside lounge seating. It is one of the most comprehensive amenity offerings among recent MBR City launches.

MBR City momentum

Mohammed Bin Rashid City is one of Dubai's fastest growing freehold districts, anchored by its lagoon communities and proximity to Downtown Dubai, Dubai Creek Harbour and Meydan One. Off plan property in MBR City has historically delivered strong capital appreciation from launch through to handover, and early stage pricing on a brand new release such as Everly Place typically offers the most attractive entry point before subsequent price releases.

Living in MBR City: What to Expect

Connectivity

Positioned close to Al Khail Road for smooth access to Downtown Dubai, Business Bay and Dubai Creek Harbour. Meydan One Mall is approximately 10 minutes away and Dubai Mall around 12 minutes.

Surrounding Community

Meydan Horizon spans over 39 million square feet and is envisioned as a mixed use district blending residential, commercial and retail spaces, with views toward the Ras Al Khor Wildlife Sanctuary.

Schools Nearby

The wider MBR City and Sobha Hartland corridor is home to a strong cluster of international schools, making the area popular with families as well as investors.

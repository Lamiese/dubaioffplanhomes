Why Buy Off Plan Property at Emaar Beachfront?

Emaar Beachfront is one of the most exclusive addresses in Dubai — a private gated island development positioned between Jumeirah Beach Residence and Palm Jumeirah, with 1.5 kilometres of pristine private white sand beach. Access is restricted to residents and their guests, creating a level of exclusivity that no other Dubai beach community can replicate.

The island sits within Dubai Harbour, home to the Middle East's largest marina, two cruise terminals and a growing roster of five-star dining and leisure. For investors, Emaar Beachfront combines the scarcity value of genuine beachfront living with Emaar's development credibility and strong rental demand from high-net-worth tenants and holiday home guests.

Rental Yields and Investment Returns

Emaar Beachfront delivers gross rental yields of 6% to 7% on a long-term basis, with short-term holiday home operators frequently achieving significantly higher returns — particularly for beach-facing units during peak seasons. The private beach access is the key differentiator, commanding a rental premium of 20% to 35% over equivalent non-beach properties in nearby JBR.

Capital appreciation has been exceptional across launched phases. Early buyers in projects such as Beach Vista, Sunrise Bay and Palace Beach Residence have seen gains of 40% to 70% from launch price to current market value, driven by scarcity, Emaar brand premium and the continued maturation of Dubai Harbour as a destination.

All off-plan properties at Emaar Beachfront — starting from AED 2 million — qualify for the UAE 10-Year Golden Visa. This grants long-term residency in the UAE for you and your immediate family, with no minimum stay requirement and no employer sponsorship needed.

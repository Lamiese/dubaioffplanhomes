Common Questions
Everything You Need to Know
What is off-plan property in Dubai?+
Off-plan property in Dubai refers to properties purchased directly from developers before or during construction. Buyers pay a deposit — typically 10% — with the remainder in instalments tied to construction milestones, often with post-handover payment plans available.
Is buying off-plan property in Dubai safe?+
Yes. Dubai's Real Estate Regulatory Authority (RERA) protects off-plan buyers through mandatory escrow accounts, ensuring developer funds are only released at verified construction milestones. Dubai is one of the most regulated off-plan markets in the world.
What is the minimum deposit for off-plan property in Dubai?+
Most developers require a minimum deposit of 10% to secure a unit. Some new launches offer as little as 5% on reservation, with the remaining balance spread across construction milestones and post-handover payment plans.
Can foreigners buy off-plan property in Dubai?+
Yes. Non-UAE nationals can freely purchase off-plan property in Dubai's designated freehold zones, which cover the majority of major communities including Downtown Dubai, Dubai Marina, Palm Jumeirah, Dubai Hills Estate, Business Bay, JVC and many more.
What is the best area to buy off-plan property in Dubai?+
The best area depends on your budget and goals. Downtown Dubai, Dubai Marina and Palm Jumeirah offer prestige and strong rental demand. Dubai Hills Estate, JVC and Emaar South offer better entry prices with solid capital appreciation. We can advise you on the right fit for your investment.
Is the service free?+
Completely. Our service is 100% free to buyers. Developers pay our fee directly, so you receive full expert guidance, property shortlisting, payment plan advice and purchase support at absolutely no cost to you.
